## Dissertation

My dissertation is included in the **MSc Computing Project** section of this repository. It focuses on the development of an **AI-powered early-warning framework** for the early detection and mitigation of delays and cost overruns in construction project management.

The study uses project-controls data and a transparent dashboard-based approach. It combines **Earned Value Management (EVM) indicators**, **interpretable scoring methods**, and **comparative AI models** to support practical and explainable decision-making.
