## Dissertation

My dissertation is included in the **MSc Computing Project** section of this repository. It focuses on the development of an **AI-powered early-warning framework** for the early detection and mitigation of delays and cost overruns in construction project management.

The study is built around project-controls data and a transparent dashboard-based approach. It combines **Earned Value Management (EVM) indicators**, **interpretable scoring methods**, and **comparative AI models** to support practical and explainable decision-making in construction projects.

## Application to Construction Project Controls

A central theme in my work is the integration of **AI methods with construction project management and project-controls practice**. This includes the use of scheduling, earned value indicators, forecasting, data analytics, and dashboard-based tools to support:

- early warning of delay and cost overrun risks
- performance monitoring and reporting
- transparent decision support
- data-informed project controls
- practical and explainable AI applications for construction environments

This direction is reflected strongly in my dissertation, which explores how AI and project-controls data can be combined to improve visibility, risk awareness, and decision-making in construction project delivery.
